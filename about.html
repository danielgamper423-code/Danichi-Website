How We Started

We got tired of the same story.

Every trade business owner we talked to had the same frustration — they'd hired a marketing agency, paid thousands a month, and had nothing to show for it. No leads. No accountability. Just monthly reports full of impressions and "brand awareness."

We saw the problem clearly: most agencies optimise for their own retention, not your results. They charge the same fee whether they deliver or not. That misalignment is why trade businesses keep getting burned.

Danichi was built on one principle — we only win when you win. No fluff metrics. No long-term lock-in contracts. Just a clear lead target, a clear cost-per-lead, and a guarantee that if we miss it, you don't pay.

We focus exclusively on trade businesses because we know the space. We know how your customers search, how they make decisions, and what it takes to get them to pick up the phone.
