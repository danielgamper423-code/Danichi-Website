Getting Started
We work exclusively with trade businesses — HVAC, plumbing, electrical, roofing, landscaping, painting, general contracting, pest control, and similar service businesses. We don't work with e-commerce, SaaS, or non-trade service businesses. This focus is intentional: we know the trades deeply, and that's why our results are consistently strong in this space.

Results & Guarantee
Before we start, we agree on a specific monthly lead target and a maximum cost-per-lead. If we hit those numbers, you pay our management fee in full. If we miss, we waive all or part of our fee proportionally — you only pay for what we actually deliver. This isn't a vague promise. It's written into our agreement so you're never paying for underperformance.
It depends on your trade, service area, and ad budget. Our clients typically see 10–40 qualified leads per month. On the strategy call we'll give you a specific projection based on your market — not a generic range. What we can tell you is our average cost per lead across all active clients is currently $14.
Most clients see their first leads within 48–72 hours of campaign launch. The first 2–3 weeks is a learning phase where we're actively optimising targeting and creative based on real data. By the end of month one, campaigns are typically hitting their cost-per-lead target consistently.
You get a weekly report showing every lead generated, the cost per lead, total ad spend, and how we're tracking against the monthly target. We set up full end-to-end tracking so you can see the journey from ad impression to phone call or form submission. No black boxes — you always know exactly where your money is going.
Campaigns & Ads
Our primary channel is Meta (Facebook & Instagram) — specifically lead generation and conversion campaigns targeting homeowners in your service area. We handle all creative (ad copy, images, video if available), audience targeting, retargeting, and ongoing A/B testing. We also offer Google Reviews campaigns and reactivation campaigns to past customers.
Three things: trade-specific audience data (we know which targeting signals convert for HVAC vs roofing vs plumbing), conversion-optimised creative (built around how trade customers make decisions, not general ad templates), and daily active management (we're in the account every day, not checking in weekly). Pricing is custom based on your trade, service area, and lead volume goals. There are two costs: our management fee (what you pay Danichi) and your ad spend (what goes directly to Meta). We'll give you the exact numbers on the strategy call. What we can say is most clients see positive ROI within the first 30 days — the leads pay for the service.
